Binding Energy dataset for NH3

Click on the ZPE corrected Binding Energy value to display on the Jmol applet, and start to use Jmol, the complex between ammonia and the water whole cluster. The Values are sorted from the lower to higher and are in kJ/mol unit.

The BE are computed at ONIOM(DLPNO-CCSD(T)/aug-cc-pvtz:xTB-GFN2)//ONIOM(B97D3/aug-cc-pvtz:xTB-GFN2) level. To see the zone treated with higher accuracy via ONIOM method click on "zoom".
